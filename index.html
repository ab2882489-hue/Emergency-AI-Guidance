Emergency Types

Choking Emergency

Step 1 of 5
1

Ask If They're Choking

Ask the person: "Are you choking?" If they can cough or speak, encourage them to keep coughing to try to dislodge the object.

2

Perform Back Blows

Stand behind them and slightly to one side. Support their chest with one hand. Lean them forward so the object blocking their airway will come out of their mouth, not move further down.

About & Disclaimer

Important information about using RescueVoice AI

Important Disclaimer

RescueVoice AI is an assistance tool only and not a substitute for professional medical care.

In any emergency, always call your local emergency number (911 in the US) first. This tool provides guidance based on common first aid protocols but cannot diagnose or treat medical conditions.

The voice guidance and step-by-step instructions are for informational purposes. Always follow the advice of trained medical professionals.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy:

  • Voice recordings are processed locally and not stored on servers
  • Location data is only used to find nearby hospitals and is not tracked
  • No personal health information is collected or shared
  • This tool works offline for basic guidance

How It Works

RescueVoice AI uses:

  • Web Speech API for voice recognition and synthesis
  • Emergency response algorithms based on Red Cross guidelines
  • Location services to find nearby medical facilities
  • Responsive design for use on any device

In a Life-Threatening Emergency

Call 911 (or your local emergency number) immediately. Do not rely solely on this app for critical emergencies.
